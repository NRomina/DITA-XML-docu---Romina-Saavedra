How to Start a Spotify Jam

Here you will find instructions about starting a Spotify Jam.

  1. Begin playing a song on your Spotify mobile app.
  2. Tap the Connect to a Device icon.
  3. Hit Start a Jam and then Share link to invite your friends to a Spotify listening party.
  4. Share the link via WhatsApp, Instragram, Chrome, etc.
Your friends can join your jam and start adding songs to the playlist for a group listening session.
