How to Synchronize with Other Devices

Here you will learn to use one device to remotely control listening on another.

When connecting to a speaker for the first time, all devices need to be on the same WiFi.
  1. Open Spotify and play something.
  2. Tap the monitor and speaker icon at the bottom-left corner.
  3. Pick the device you want to play on.
You can use your phone to control Spotify on the other device
