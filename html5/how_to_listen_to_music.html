How to Listen to Music

Here you will learn how to listen to music using the Spotify app.

To listen to music with Spotify and access the best functionalities of the app, you must download it in your phone, create an account and pay for the premium version.
  1. Tap on the Spotify icon on your phone menu.
  2. Hit the Search icon at the bottom of the screen.
  3. On the search bar, type the name of the song, album or artist/band you want to listen to and hit Search.
  4. A list will appear on the screen for you to choose from playlists, albums or radio stations. Tap the one you wish to listen to.
  5. Hit the Play icon on the chosen playlist, album or radio station.
The playlist starts to run.
