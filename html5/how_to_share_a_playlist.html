How to Share a Playlist

In this section, you will learn how to share a playlist with your friends.

  1. Tap Your Library on the main screen.
  2. Choose the playlist you want to share.
  3. Tap the Share icon and then Copy link.
  4. Hit the Share icon and paste the link via WhatsApp, Instagram, Chrome, etc.
Your friend(s) can open the playlist on their phones, follow it and listen to it.
