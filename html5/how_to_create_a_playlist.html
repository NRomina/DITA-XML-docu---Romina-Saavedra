How to Create a Playlist

Here you will learn how to create a playlist on Spotify.

  1. Tap Your Library at the bottom-left corner of the main screen.
  2. Hit the plus (+) sign at the top-right corner of Your Library and choose playlist.
  3. Give you playlist a name and hit Create.
  4. To add songs to your playlist, use the search bar to look for the songs you like.
  5. Hit the three dots next to the song and tap Add to playlist in the menu that appears.
  6. Choose the name of the playlist you just created and tap Create.
  7. Repeat steps 4, 5 and 6 for as many songs you wish to add to your playlist.
The playlist is ready to run in Your Library.
